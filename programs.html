Our Academic Programs

Diploma in Nursing

This comprehensive program prepares students to become registered nurses, capable of providing holistic patient care. The curriculum integrates theoretical knowledge with extensive clinical practice in various healthcare settings. Graduates are equipped to work in hospitals, clinics, community health centers, and other healthcare facilities.

Key Modules:

  • Anatomy and Physiology
  • Pharmacology
  • Medical-Surgical Nursing
  • Pediatric Nursing
  • Community Health Nursing
  • Mental Health Nursing

Diploma in Midwifery

Our Diploma in Midwifery program focuses on the specialized care of women during pregnancy, childbirth, and the postnatal period, as well as newborn care. Students develop expertise in antenatal care, labor and delivery management, family planning, and reproductive health services.

Key Modules:

  • Reproductive Health
  • Antenatal Care
  • Intrapartum Care (Labor and Delivery)
  • Postnatal Care
  • Neonatal Care
  • Community Midwifery

Certificate in Nursing

The Certificate in Nursing program provides foundational knowledge and practical skills for entry-level nursing roles. This program is ideal for individuals seeking to quickly enter the healthcare field and provide essential patient support under supervision. It serves as a stepping stone for further nursing education.

Key Modules:

  • Basic Nursing Skills
  • First Aid and Emergency Care
  • Hygiene and Infection Control
  • Patient Communication
Apply Now

Certificate in Midwifery

Our Certificate in Midwifery provides fundamental training in midwifery practices, focusing on basic maternal and newborn care. This program is designed to equip students with the necessary skills to assist in safe deliveries and provide primary care in various settings, particularly in rural and underserved areas.

Key Modules:

  • Basic Midwifery Skills
  • Maternal Health Basics
  • Newborn Care Basics
  • Family Planning Services
