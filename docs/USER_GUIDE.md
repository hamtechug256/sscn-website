# 🎯 SSCN Website - User Guide for Non-Technical Staff

## Making Content Management EASY for Everyone

---

## 📝 QUICK START GUIDE

### How to Login
1. Go to: `yoursite.com/admin`
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Login"

### Main Dashboard
After login, you'll see:
- **Statistics** - How many articles, events, etc.
- **Recent Activity** - Latest contact messages
- **Quick Actions** - Shortcuts to add content

---

## 📰 ADDING A NEWS ARTICLE

### Step-by-Step

1. **Click "News & Updates"** in the left sidebar
2. **Click "Add News Article"** button (blue button, top right)
3. **Fill in the form:**
   - **Title** - The headline (required)
   - **Image** - Drag and drop a photo, or click to browse
   - **Excerpt** - A short summary (shown on news listing)
   - **Content** - The full article (use formatting buttons)
   - **Category** - Select from dropdown
   - **Featured** - Turn ON to highlight on homepage
   - **Published** - Turn ON to make it live
4. **Click "Save Article"**

### Tips for News Articles
- ✅ Keep titles under 60 characters
- ✅ Use clear, simple language
- ✅ Add a relevant image
- ✅ Preview before publishing
- ✅ Write a short excerpt (2-3 sentences)

---

## 📅 ADDING AN EVENT

### Step-by-Step

1. **Click "Events"** in the left sidebar
2. **Click "Add Event"** button
3. **Fill in the form:**
   - **Title** - Event name
   - **Date** - When is the event
   - **Time** - What time
   - **Location** - Where
   - **Description** - Event details
   - **Image** - Event poster/photo
4. **Click "Save Event"**

---

## 👨‍🏫 ADDING A FACULTY MEMBER

### Step-by-Step

1. **Click "Faculty"** in the left sidebar
2. **Click "Add Faculty"** button
3. **Fill in the form:**
   - **Name** - Full name
   - **Title** - Position (e.g., "Senior Tutor")
   - **Department** - Nursing, Midwifery, etc.
   - **Bio** - Short biography
   - **Photo** - Portrait photo
   - **Email** - Work email (optional)
   - **Phone** - Work phone (optional)
4. **Click "Save"**

### Tips for Faculty Photos
- ✅ Use professional headshots
- ✅ Square images work best
- ✅ Clear, well-lit photos
- ❌ No group photos
- ❌ No casual/selfie style photos

---

## 🖼️ UPLOADING IMAGES

### How to Upload
1. **Drag and Drop** - Drag image onto upload area
2. **OR Click to Browse** - Select file from your computer

### Accepted File Types
- JPG / JPEG (recommended for photos)
- PNG (for graphics/logos)
- WebP (modern format)

### Image Size Limits
- Maximum: 5MB per image
- Recommended: Under 1MB for faster loading

### Tips for Good Images
- ✅ Clear, high-quality images
- ✅ Relevant to the content
- ✅ Proper orientation (not sideways)
- ❌ No blurry or dark images
- ❌ No copyrighted images without permission

---

## 💬 VIEWING CONTACT MESSAGES

### How to Check Messages
1. **Click "Inquiries"** in the left sidebar
2. Messages are listed with:
   - Sender name
   - Email address
   - Subject
   - Date received
   - Status (Read/Unread)

### Status Colors
- 🔴 **Red dot** = Unread (new message)
- ⚪ **Gray** = Read
- ✅ **Green** = Replied

### Responding to Messages
- Click on message to view full details
- Reply directly via email (copy email address)

## ❓ COMMON QUESTIONS

### "I made a mistake, how do I edit?"
1. Find the item in the list
2. Click the **pencil icon** (edit)
3. Make your changes
4. Click "Save"

### "How do I delete something?"
1. Find the item in the list
2. Click the **trash icon** (delete)
3. Confirm deletion in the popup
4. ⚠️ **Warning: Deletion is permanent!**

### "Can I undo a deletion?"
- Unfortunately, no. Once deleted, content is gone.
- **Tip:** Unpublish instead of delete to keep content safe.

### "What does 'Published' mean?"
- **ON** = Visible to public on the website
- **OFF** = Saved as draft, not visible to public

### "What does 'Featured' mean?"
- Featured items appear in special highlighted sections
- Example: Featured news appears prominently on homepage
