About Our Institution

Learn about our history, mission, and commitment to excellence in healthcare education.

Our Story

Soroti School of Comprehensive Nursing is a beacon of excellence in healthcare education, nestled in the vibrant heart of Soroti City, Uganda. Established with a vision to address the critical need for skilled and compassionate healthcare professionals, our institution has grown to become a leading training ground for nurses and midwives.

Our commitment extends beyond academic rigor. We believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who are not only proficient in their medical skills but also deeply empathetic and dedicated to serving their communities. Our curriculum is meticulously designed to blend theoretical knowledge with extensive practical experience, ensuring our graduates are ready to face the challenges of the real-world healthcare environment.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, comprehensive nursing and midwifery education that fosters professional competence, ethical conduct, and a commitment to community health and well-being.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in healthcare education, recognized for producing innovative, compassionate, and globally competent nursing and midwifery professionals.

Our Core Values

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in education and practice.

Compassion

Demonstrating empathy and care for all individuals.

Integrity

Upholding honesty, respect, and ethical principles.

Innovation

Embracing new knowledge and practices in healthcare.

Community Service

Dedication to improving the health of the wider community.

About SSCN

The Soroti School of Comprehensive Nursing is dedicated to training the next generation of healthcare leaders in Uganda through excellence in education, research, and clinical practice.
