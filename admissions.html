Admissions

Admission Requirements

Prospective students must meet the following minimum requirements for admission to our diploma and certificate programs:

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) or its equivalent with at least 5 passes in English, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics.
  • Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) or its equivalent with at least 2 principal passes in Biology and Chemistry.
  • For Certificate programs, relevant prior learning or experience may be considered.
  • Applicants must be at least 18 years of age at the time of admission.
  • Successful completion of an oral interview and written entrance examination.

Application Process

  1. Obtain Application Form: Application forms can be downloaded from our website or picked up from the school's admissions office during working hours.
  2. Complete Form: Fill out the application form accurately and completely. Ensure all required sections are addressed.
  3. Attach Documents: Submit certified copies of your academic transcripts, certificates, national identification, and two passport-sized photographs.
  4. Pay Application Fee: A non-refundable application fee must be paid to the school's designated bank account. Details are provided on the application form.
  5. Submit Application: Return the completed form and all attachments to the admissions office by the specified deadline.
  6. Entrance Examination & Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an entrance examination and an oral interview.
  7. Admission Offer: Successful candidates will receive an official admission offer letter.
