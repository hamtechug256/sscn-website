About Us

H&B Metal Workshop is a family-run business specializing in custom steel, bronze, brass, and stainless steel windows and doors. Our products are handcrafted in Italy and designed for modern luxury homes.

We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to create architectural elements that are not only visually stunning but also exceptionally durable and functional.

  • ✔️ Decades of metal fabrication expertise
  • ✔️ Precision engineering and artistic design
  • ✔️ Family-owned, customer-focused values
