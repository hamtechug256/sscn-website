Admissions

Welcome to the Admissions page of Soroti School of Comprehensive Nursing. We are excited that you are considering joining our community of future healthcare professionals.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of academic transcripts
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement
  • Proof of English proficiency (if applicable)

Application Process

To apply, please follow these steps:

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Submit all required documents.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Attend an interview (if required).
  5. Await notification of admission decision.

Important Dates

Make sure to keep track of the following important dates:

  • Application Deadline: June 30, 2025
  • Interviews: July 15-20, 2025
  • Notification of Admission: August 1, 2025
