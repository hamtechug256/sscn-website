Campus Life at Soroti School of Comprehensive Nursing

At SSCN, we believe that education extends beyond the classroom. Our vibrant campus life is designed to foster personal growth, community engagement, and lifelong friendships.

Student Activities

Students can participate in various clubs and organizations, including:

  • Health and Wellness Club
  • Student Government Association
  • Cultural Exchange Programs
  • Sports Teams

Events and Traditions

Throughout the academic year, SSCN hosts numerous events that bring the community together:

  • Annual Health Fair
  • Graduation Ceremony
  • Inter-School Sports Competitions
  • Community Service Days

Living on Campus

Our campus offers comfortable and affordable housing options, with amenities designed to support student life:

  • 24/7 Security
  • Study Lounges
  • Recreational Facilities
  • Cafeteria with Diverse Meal Options
