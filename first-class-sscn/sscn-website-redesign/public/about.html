About Soroti School of Comprehensive Nursing

Soroti School of Comprehensive Nursing is dedicated to training competent and compassionate healthcare professionals. Our mission is to provide high-quality education in nursing and midwifery, fostering a learning environment that encourages critical thinking and practical expertise.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in nursing education, recognized for excellence in training and community service.

Our Values

  • Integrity
  • Compassion
  • Excellence
  • Innovation

Why Choose Us?

We offer experienced faculty, modern facilities, and extensive clinical exposure to ensure our students are well-prepared for their careers in healthcare.
