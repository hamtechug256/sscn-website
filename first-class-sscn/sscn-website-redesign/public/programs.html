Our Academic Programs

At Soroti School of Comprehensive Nursing, we offer a range of programs designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary for a successful career in healthcare.

Available Programs

Diploma in Nursing

This program prepares students for a rewarding career in nursing, focusing on both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

Diploma in Midwifery

Our Midwifery program provides specialized training in maternal and child health, preparing students for impactful roles in healthcare.

Certificate in Nursing

This entry-level program offers foundational knowledge and skills for those looking to start a career in nursing.

Certificate in Midwifery

This program provides essential training in midwifery practices, preparing students for various roles in maternal care.
