Campus Life

Student Accommodation

We offer comfortable and secure on-campus accommodation for students, providing a conducive environment for study and rest.

Recreational Activities

Beyond academics, students have opportunities to participate in various recreational activities, including sports, cultural events, and clubs.

Student Support Services

We are committed to the well-being of our students. Our support services include academic counseling, career guidance, and health services.

Community Engagement

Students are encouraged to participate in community health outreach programs, gaining practical experience while contributing to the health and well-being of the local community.
