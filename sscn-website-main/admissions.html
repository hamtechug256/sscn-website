Admissions

Begin your journey to becoming a healthcare professional at SSCN.

Admission Requirements

Prospective students must meet the following minimum requirements for admission to our diploma programs:

Academic Qualifications (UCE)

Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) or its equivalent with at least 5 passes in English, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics.

Academic Qualifications (UACE)

Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) or its equivalent with at least 2 principal passes in Biology and Chemistry.

Age Requirement

Applicants must be at least 18 years of age at the time of admission.

Entrance Examination & Interview

Successful completion of an oral interview and written entrance examination is mandatory.

Application Process

1

Obtain Application Form

Application forms can be downloaded from our website or picked up from the school's admissions office during working hours.

2

Complete Form

Fill out the application form accurately and completely. Ensure all required sections are addressed.

3

Attach Documents

Submit certified copies of your academic transcripts, certificates, national identification, and two passport-sized photographs.

4

Pay Application Fee

A non-refundable application fee must be paid to the school's designated bank account. Details are provided on the application form.

5

Submit Application

Return the completed form and all attachments to the admissions office by the specified deadline.

6

Entrance Exam & Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an entrance examination and an oral interview.

7

Admission Offer

Successful candidates will receive an official admission offer letter.
